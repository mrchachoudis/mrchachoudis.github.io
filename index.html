§ 02 Capabilities what I do

What I actually do.

Six honest answers to “so what can you do?” — the things I’ll happily get into a long argument about.

α

Applied ML, end-to-end

Python · scikit-learn · PyTorch · SHAP. Take a problem from raw data through baselines, interpretation, and a defensible writeup. Bias toward simple models that are explainable.

β

Bioinformatics & fisheries

eDNA / COI sequence work, BLAST pipelines, stock assessment, panel regression. Comfortable with messy field data and the statistical structures it lives inside.

γ

Shipping product

WordPress + custom PHP, native Kotlin Android, Firebase, REST APIs, Java/JS bridges. End-to-end stack ownership including the unsexy operational parts.

δ

Self-hosted infrastructure

Proxmox homelab. Nextcloud, Jellyfin, WireGuard, Pi-hole, SearXNG. A local LLM stack on RTX 5060 Ti running Qwen3-14B for agentic experiments.

ε

Data analysis

52,000-row Greek fisheries dataset cleaned and analysed for my thesis. Mann-Kendall, STL, K-means, PCA, Shannon, SST correlation. Tables that hold up to review.

ζ

Writing under difficulty

Co-author on multiple in-flight papers. The skill I care about most: explaining a hard result clearly, without lying about how clean the road there was.
